Chicken Basil

Description

Another easy recipe that would make restaurants go bankrupt!

This easy to make chicken recipe has a subtle minty flavor that's full of protein. Perfect for fitness enthusiasts!

Ingredients

  • 1/2lb Chicken Thigh with skin
  • 1/4cup Basil coarse or 5 leaves Basil
  • 1 clove Garlic
  • 3 tbsp Ginger
  • 1/2 Chicken bouillon
  • 1 tbsp Pepper
  • 1 sachet Oyster Sauce
  • 3 tbsp Oil

Steps

  1. Dice the chicken bite size with its skin intact and set aside.
  2. If using Basil leaves, chopped it coarsely. Chop Garlic and Ginger finely.
  3. Put oil on a pan, saute garlic and ginger. Once garlic is light brownish, put the chicken and mix.
  4. After 10 minutes or when the chicken starts to brown, mix the bouillon in 1/2 cup of hot water and pour it on the pan.
  5. Add pepper. Let it simmer for 20 more minutes or until there's only a little bit of liquid left.
  6. Add oyster sauce and mix. Turn off the heat and add basil. Keep on mixing until satisfied. Enjoy!
