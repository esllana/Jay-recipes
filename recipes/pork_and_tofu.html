Pork and Tofu

Description

A simple pork and tofu dish with a good blend of sourness and saltiness sprinkled with spring onions. A perfect dish for beer nights with your buddies.

Ingredients

  • 200g Pork Belly (sliced)
  • 100g Tofu (cubed)
  • 1 clove Garlic (chopped, fine)
  • 2 cloves Red Onion (chopped, strand)
  • 2 tbsp Brown sugar
  • 1 tsp Pepper
  • 1/2 cup Soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup Vinegar
  • 1/2 cup Water
  • 4 leaves Spring Onion (finely chopped)
  • 5tbsp Oil

Steps

  1. On a pan, fry the tofu until the skin is semi crispy. make sure that a good portion of it is still soft. Set aside
  2. Slice the pork belly intto 1-2 inch long slices then fry. It is preferred to deep fry for extra crispiness but pan fried works just as well. Set aside and save the oils and rendered fats.
  3. On a bowl mix the soy sauce, vinegar, and water to create our sauce.
  4. Mix the raw garlic, raw onion, brown sugar, pepper, oils, and fats together with our sauce. Put on a pan and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Add the spring onions for the finishing touch and voila!
